Frostburg State University - Academic Buildings

This page of the websites lists all of the Academic buildings here at Frostburg State.

Each building will include its name, a short description, and some will include a picture.

Academic Buildings:

Center for Communications and Information Technology (CCIT):

This building mainly includes Computer Science, Engineering, Cybersecurity, and Math classes.

Education and Health Science Center (EHSC):

Mainly deals with Health Science classes, Exercise Science classes, other science classes, as well as Parks and Recreation and Education Classes.

Also one of the newest buildings on campus.

Fine Arts Building:

Mainly deals with classes of the Arts.

Dunkle Hall:

Mainly deals with History, English, and some Business classes, and mostly general education classes.

Gunter Hall:

Mainly deals with Geography classes and labs.

Compton Science Center:

Mainly deals with other Science Classes like biology, chemistry, physics, as well as Engineering classes.

Guild Human Resources Center:

Deals mainly with sociology and psychology classes.

Lowndes Hall:

Also mainly deals with psychology classes.
