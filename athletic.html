Athletic Buildings:

Cordts PE Center:

Is a center connected to the lane center that includes a gym, the wrestling room, auxiliary gyms, Bobcat Arena, a rock wall, a swimming pool and the locker rooms for the mens and womens teams.

Bobcat Arena:

Is an indoor small-scale arena at Frostburg that hosts home indoor events like mens/womens wrestling matches, mens/womens basketball games, and acrobatics & tumbling meets.

Bobcat Stadium:

Is an outdoor turf field that hosts home football games, home mens/womens soccer matches, home field hockey games, home womens/mens lacrosse games, and home track & field events.

Baseball Field:

Hosts where the softball and baseball team practice and have there home games at.

Rugby Pitch:

Where the mens and womens team practice and have there home games at.

Tennis/Pickleball Courts:

Courts where the Tennis teams practice at and also where people can play tennis and pickleball at.

Soccer Fields:

Where the mens and womens soccer teams practice at.
