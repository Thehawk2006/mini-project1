Frostburg State University - Residence Halls and Dining Options

This page lists the residence halls and dining options located at Frostburg State University.

Each hall and dining option includes its name and a short description of what it offers.

Residence Halls:

Annapolis Hall:

A traditional residence hall offering double/single rooms. It primarily houses first-year students.

Cumberland Hall

Residence hall consisisting of only single rooms.

Allen Hall:

A traditional residence hall offering double rooms and community-style bathrooms. It primarily houses first-year students.

Diehl Hall:

A co-ed residence hall with double rooms and shared bathroom facilities. Located conveniently near academic buildings and dining areas.

Frederick Hall:

One of the suite-style residence halls.

Gray Hall:

A traditional-style residence hall primarily for first-year students. Offers a close-knit community atmosphere.

Simpson Hall:

A suite-style residence hall offering modern amenities and shared living spaces.

Westminster Hall:

A residence hall designed primarily for upperclassmen.

Dining Options:

Chesapeake Dining Hall:

A buffet-style dining hall option that allows students all you can eat with a meal swipe.

Lane Center:

Offers a variety of dining options that include Chickfila, Starbucks, a mexican-style place, a greek-style place, an italian pizza and sandwhich place, and a all american diner place.

Firehouse Subs:

A sub chain located in Sowers hall.
